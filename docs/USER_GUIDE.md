# Rituals - User Guide

**Welcome to Rituals!** This guide will help you get started with building your daily ritual habits.

---

## What is Rituals?

Rituals is a simple app that helps you remember and perform your daily routines. Set up your morning meditation, evening journaling, or any other ritual, and get gentle reminders at the right time.

---

## Getting Started

### First Launch

1. Open the Rituals app
2. You'll see an empty state with a sunrise icon
3. Tap the **+** button (bottom-right) to create your first ritual

---

## Creating a Ritual

### Step 1: Tap the + Button
The floating **+** button is always available on the ritual list screen.

### Step 2: Fill in the Details

**Title** (Required)
- Give your ritual a clear, short name
- Examples: "Morning Meditation", "Evening Journaling", "Lunch Break Walk"
- Maximum 60 characters

**Description** (Optional)
- Add details about what to do
- Examples: 
  - "Sit quietly for 10 minutes. Focus on breathing."
  - "Write 3 things you're grateful for today."
- Maximum 500 characters

**Time** (Required)
- Tap the time display to open the time picker
- Select when you want to be reminded
- The app will show you how long until the next notification

### Step 3: Save
- Tap **Save** in the top-right corner
- Your ritual appears in the list
- A notification is automatically scheduled

## Using Your Rituals

### Viewing the List

All your rituals appear on the main screen, sorted by time (earliest first).

Each ritual card shows:
- **Title** â€” The ritual name
- **Time** â€” When you'll be reminded (e.g., "8:30 AM")
- **Edit button** â€” Tap the pencil icon to modify

### When a Notification Arrives

1. You'll receive a notification at your ritual's scheduled time
2. The notification shows your ritual title
3. **Tap the notification** to open the ritual detail screen

### Completing a Ritual

1. Open the ritual (tap card or notification)
2. Read your ritual description
3. Perform your ritual
4. Tap **Mark as Done**
5. A green checkmark animation appears
6. You return to the list automatically

**Note:** The "Done" button is for visual confirmation only. Completion history is not tracked in the MVP version.

---

## Editing a Ritual

### Option 1: From the List
1. Tap the **pencil icon** on any ritual card
2. Make your changes
3. Tap **Save**

### Option 2: From Detail Screen
1. Tap a ritual card to open details
2. Tap the **edit icon** (top-right)
3. Make your changes
4. Tap **Save**

**What you can change:**
- Title
- Description
- Time (notification will be rescheduled automatically)

---

## Deleting a Ritual

### Option 1: Swipe to Delete
1. Swipe a ritual card **from right to left**
2. A red delete button appears
3. Confirm deletion in the dialog

### Option 2: Delete Button
1. Open the edit screen for a ritual
2. Scroll to the bottom
3. Tap **Delete Ritual**
4. Confirm deletion in the dialog

**Warning:** Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. The scheduled notification will also be cancelled.

## Understanding Notifications

### How Notifications Work

- **Daily reminders:** Notifications repeat every day at your chosen time
- **Local only:** No internet required; notifications work offline
- **Tap to open:** Tapping a notification opens the ritual detail screen

### Platform-Specific Notes

**iOS:**
- You'll be asked for notification permission on first launch
- Grant permission to receive reminders
- Manage permissions in Settings â†’ Rituals â†’ Notifications

**macOS:**
- Similar to iOS, permission required
- Native macOS notifications appear in Notification Center
- Manage in System Settings â†’ Notifications â†’ Rituals

**Web:**
- Notifications are **not supported** in the MVP version
- You can still use the app to manage rituals
- Check the app manually to see your ritual list

## Tips for Success

### Start Small
- Begin with 1-2 rituals
- Add more as habits form

### Be Specific
- Write clear descriptions
- Include duration (e.g., "10 minutes")
- Add step-by-step instructions if helpful

### Time It Right
- Choose realistic times when you're available
- Consider your daily schedule
- Morning rituals: 6:00-9:00 AM
- Evening rituals: 7:00-10:00 PM

### Ritual Ideas

**Morning:**
- Meditation (5-10 minutes)
- Gratitude journaling
- Stretching routine
- Healthy breakfast reminder

**Afternoon:**
- Lunch break walk
- Hydration check
- Posture reset

**Evening:**
- Daily review/journaling
- Reading time
- Screen-free wind-down
- Tomorrow's preparation

## Troubleshooting

### Not Receiving Notifications?

**iOS/macOS:**
1. Check Settings â†’ Rituals â†’ Notifications
2. Ensure "Allow Notifications" is ON
3. Try deleting and recreating the ritual

**Web:**
- Notifications aren't supported yet
- Check the app manually

### Ritual Not Appearing?

1. Pull down on the list to refresh
2. Check if it was accidentally deleted
3. Restart the app

### Wrong Time Showing?

- The app uses your device's local time
- Check your device time zone settings
- Edit the ritual to update the time

### App Crashing?

1. Restart the app
2. Update to the latest version
3. Report the issue with details about what you were doing
